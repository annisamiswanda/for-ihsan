sayang, i've been thinking about the other night. honestly, aku ngerasa bersalah karena udah terlalu larut sama pikiranku sendiri sampai ga fokus pas ketemu kamu. waktu aku memilih buat nyimpen semuanya sendiri, ternyata bukan cuma aku yang ga nyaman, you felt it too. kamu langsung sadar ada sesuatu yang beda dari aku, dan aku tau itu bikin kamu ikut ga tenang, and i don't want to do that to you again. aku belajar kalau aku ga harus figure everything out sendiri dulu sebelum cerita ke kamu. aku ga harus nunggu sampai semua pikiran di kepala aku beres. i can just talk to you, 'cause every time i do, things somehow feel lighter. having someone who genuinely cares about how i feel has been such a blessing in my life. i've spent so much of my life carrying things on my own that sometimes i still forget i don't have to anymore. tapi semakin aku pikirin, aku sadar kalau yang paling membekas dari malam itu bukan soal apa yang kita bahas. it's the way you love me. aku merasa dicintai bukan cuma dari hal-hal besar, tapi justru dari hal-hal kecil yang kamu lakuin setiap hari dan mungkin bahkan ga kamu sadari. dari cara kamu langsung notice kalau ada sesuatu yang beda dari aku, selalu berusaha ngerti apa yang sebenarnya aku rasain sebelum ngambil kesimpulan, ga takut negur atau marahin aku kalau memang aku salah, karena aku tau itu datang dari tempat yang baik, dan kamu selalu mempertimbangkan perasaan aku dalam setiap keputusan yang kamu ambil. aku tau ga semua hal harus selalu sesuai sama apa yang aku mau. but what means the most to me is that you always make me feel like my feelings matter. bahkan saat kita punya cara pandang yang berbeda, kamu ga pernah bikin aku merasa berlebihan, terlalu sensitif, atau ga penting. instead, you always choose to understand me first. kamu selalu make me feel considered, safe, and loved. thank you for being patient with me that night, for always trying to understand me, and for caring about my feelings the way you do. i love you sooo much, sayangku. and i want you to know that i'll do the same for you. 'cause we're a team, and i never want you to carry the weight of my silence alone again. i want to be the kind of person for you that you've been for me. someone you can lean on, talk to, and feel safe with, even on the messy days. and no matter what, i'll always be grateful that i get to love you and be loved by you. thank you for finding me, and for making me realize that love doesn't have to feel lonely. 🤍 love, caca 🤍
